MERIDIAN HEALTH PLAN — MEMBER HANDBOOK
======================================

Section 1: Plan Overview
------------------------
The Meridian Choice PPO plan gives members access to a nationwide network of
in-network providers. Members may also see out-of-network providers at a higher
cost share. This handbook summarizes benefits, cost shares, and how to check the
status of a submitted claim. It is informational and is not a determination of
coverage or eligibility for any specific service.

Section 2: Cost Shares
----------------------
In-network primary care office visits have a $25 copay. In-network specialist
visits have a $50 copay. The annual in-network deductible is $1,500 per
individual and $3,000 per family. After the deductible is met, the plan pays 80%
of the allowed amount for most covered services and the member pays 20%
coinsurance until the out-of-pocket maximum is reached.

Section 3: Out-of-Pocket Maximum
--------------------------------
The annual in-network out-of-pocket maximum is $6,000 per individual and $12,000
per family. Once a member reaches the out-of-pocket maximum, the plan pays 100%
of the allowed amount for covered in-network services for the rest of the plan
year. Copays, deductible, and coinsurance count toward the out-of-pocket maximum.

Section 4: Preventive Care
--------------------------
In-network preventive care — annual wellness visits, routine immunizations, and
recommended screenings — is covered at 100% with no copay, no coinsurance, and is
not subject to the deductible. Services that are diagnostic rather than preventive
may be subject to normal cost shares.

Section 5: Prescription Drugs
-----------------------------
The plan uses a four-tier formulary. Tier 1 (generic) drugs have a $10 copay,
Tier 2 (preferred brand) a $35 copay, Tier 3 (non-preferred brand) a $70 copay,
and Tier 4 (specialty) 25% coinsurance up to $250 per fill. Mail-order supplies
of up to 90 days are available for maintenance medications.

Section 6: Claims and Reimbursement
-----------------------------------
In-network providers submit claims directly to the plan. For out-of-network or
paper claims, members submit an itemized claim form within 365 days of the date
of service. Processed claims show an allowed amount, the plan's payment, and the
member's responsibility on the Explanation of Benefits (EOB). To check the status
of a submitted claim, members provide the claim ID; status values are Received,
In Review, Approved, Denied, or Paid.

Section 7: Eligibility and Determinations
-----------------------------------------
Whether a specific service is medically necessary, covered, or eligible under the
plan is a determination made by the plan's clinical and benefits reviewers, not by
this handbook or by an automated assistant. Members and providers may request a
coverage determination or appeal a denial through the member portal.

Section 8: Contacting Member Services
-------------------------------------
Member Services is available Monday through Friday, 08:00–20:00, via the member
portal or members@meridian.example.com. For claim questions, have the claim ID
and date of service ready.
