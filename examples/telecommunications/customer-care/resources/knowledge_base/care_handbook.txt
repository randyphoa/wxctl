NORTHWIND MOBILE — CUSTOMER CARE HANDBOOK
=========================================

Section 1: Plans Overview
-------------------------
Northwind Mobile offers three postpaid plans. Essential includes 10 GB of
high-speed data per month. Plus includes 50 GB. Unlimited includes unlimited
high-speed data with mobile-hotspot allowances. All plans include unlimited
talk and text within the home country.

Section 2: Data Usage and Overage
---------------------------------
On the Essential and Plus plans, once the monthly high-speed data allowance is
used, speeds are reduced (throttled) to 512 kbps for the remainder of the
billing cycle; service is not cut off and there are no per-gigabyte overage
charges. Members can buy a high-speed data add-on for the current cycle from the
care portal. Unlimited-plan customers may be deprioritized during congestion
after 50 GB but are not throttled to a fixed speed.

Section 3: International Roaming
-------------------------------
Roaming is off by default and must be activated before travel. Members enable
roaming in the care portal or by contacting Care; activation typically applies
within one hour. A daily international roaming pass provides a set high-speed
data allowance plus talk and text in covered countries; the pass activates the
first day the device connects to a partner network abroad. Coverage and rates
vary by destination.

Section 4: No-Signal Troubleshooting
------------------------------------
If a device shows no signal or no service, follow these steps in order:
(1) toggle airplane mode on for ten seconds, then off;
(2) restart the device;
(3) confirm the SIM or eSIM is active in device settings;
(4) check for a carrier-settings or software update;
(5) if still no service, the account may have roaming disabled or be in a
coverage gap — verify roaming status and current coverage.
If none of these restore service, contact Care for a network check.

Section 5: Billing and Payments
-------------------------------
Bills are issued monthly. Autopay applies a small monthly discount. Members can
view current usage, recent invoices, and payment history in the care portal.
A late payment may result in temporary suspension until the balance is brought
current; service is restored automatically after payment posts.

Section 6: Privacy of Subscriber Data
-------------------------------------
Care agents and assistants disclose only the plan and usage details for the
subscriber the caller has identified, and only what is needed to answer the
question. They do not reveal a subscriber's stored personal details — full name,
billing address, payment instruments, or other line identifiers — beyond what the
caller already supplied. Identity verification follows the standard Care script
before any account change.

Section 7: Contacting Care
--------------------------
Care is available daily, 07:00–22:00, via the care portal or
care@northwind.example.com. Have the subscriber's mobile number (MSISDN) ready;
demonstration numbers in this handbook use the reserved 555 range, e.g.
+1-555-0142.
